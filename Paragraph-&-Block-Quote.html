THE TELANGANA

The Freedom Fighters of Telangana were a group of men and women who fought for India's independence from British rule. They hailed from all walks of life and played an essential role in the freedom struggle of India. The freedom fighters of Telangana contributed to various movements and uprisings, such as the Quit India movement, the Telangana Armed Struggle, and the Razakar movement.

Telangna state bird

Due to its legendary significance, the Palapitta, or Indian Roller, is the state bird of Telangana. According to Hindu mythology, seeing this bird on Dussehra or before any task paves the way to success. This idea is supported by the fact that Lord Ram noticed the Indian roller before attacking Lanka and triumphing. The bird was designated as Telangana's state symbol and is now regarded as a lucky omen.
